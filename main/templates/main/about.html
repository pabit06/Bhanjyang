Our Story

Established in 2069 B.S., Bhanjyang Saving & Credit Cooperative Society Ltd. was founded with a clear vision: to empower the local communities of Rupa Rural Municipality, Kaski. We began our journey with a handful of dedicated members, committed to the principles of mutual cooperation and sustainable development.

Over the years, we have grown steadily, adapting to the evolving needs of our members and the region. Our focus has always been on providing accessible, reliable, and fair financial services that contribute directly to the economic well-being and social upliftment of our community.
